Fishing Report

🎯 Bite of the Day

Low-water morning window on Taneycomo is prime — get on the water before 7 AM with micro jigs under a float for 40+ fish days. On Table Rock, sunrise topwater with Whopper Ploppers on main lake points before the heat index hits 104°F.

🏞️ Table Rock Lake ★★★★☆

Bass: Topwater action at dawn on main lake points — Whopper Ploppers, walking baits, buzzbaits. After sun climbs, go deep with crankbaits and jigs in 15–25 ft.

Bluegill: ★★★★★ Excellent! Swarming shallows — perfect for kids on vacation with live crickets on light tackle.

Walleye & Crappie: Fair-to-good around brush piles and deep points.

🌡️ Water: 78°F 📏 Level: 917.0 ft 💧 Clarity: Clear

🌊 Lake Taneycomo ★★★★☆

Morning: Low generation before 7 AM — prime wading and dock fishing. Micro jigs in olive, gray, brown under a float. 2 lb test or 6x tippet in the clear water.

Hot spot: Branson Landing area! Stocked rainbows hanging in eddies — eager to eat almost anything. Pier dock across from Scotty's Dock producing on spoons.

🌡️ Water: 48–52°F 💧 Clarity: Very clear 📈 Gen peak: ~12,800 cfs midday
