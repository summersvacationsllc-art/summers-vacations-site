🎣 Patterns & Hot Spots

Table Rock Lake

Top baits: Jighead minnow (#1), topwater at dawn, deep crankbaits 20-35 ft, football jigs on rock piles.

Hot areas: Cape Fair / James River arm, Big Indian Creek, Aunts Creek, Long Creek arm, main lake points near the dam.

Guide note: "If you're not fishing early (before 8 AM) or deep (20-35 ft), you're leaving fish on the table."

Lake Taneycomo

Hot sections: Dam to Fall Creek (fly-fishing trophy zone), Scotty's Trout Dock / Branson Landing area (surprisingly hot — 25+ fish days from pontoon).

Top methods: Pink worm under float, nightcrawlers drifting, Provo Rig (scud + egg fly). Use 2 lb test in low water.

Notable catches: 24", 6.12 lb rainbow (June/July). 19.5" rainbow at Scotty's. Multiple 40+ trout days reported.

🎣 Bottom Line: Excellent day on both lakes! Table Rock: early topwater for bass, walleye on gravel points. Taneycomo: morning no-generation window is prime — get out before 8 AM! When generation starts, switch to drifting — trout turn on when water moves.
